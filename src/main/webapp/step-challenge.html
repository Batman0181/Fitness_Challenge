Step Challenge

Walk 10,000 steps a day for 30 days and improve your health.

Why Steps Matter

Walking is a simple way to stay active. By taking 10,000 steps daily, you'll improve your cardiovascular health, boost your energy, and reduce stress levels.

Tips for Success

  • Use a step-tracking app or wearable device.
  • Take short walks during breaks.
  • Walk with a friend or family member for motivation.
