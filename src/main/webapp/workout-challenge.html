Benefits of Daily Workouts

Daily workouts improve your strength, flexibility, and endurance. Regular exercise also enhances your mood and sleep quality.

Workout Ideas

  • Bodyweight exercises (squats, push-ups, planks).
  • Yoga or stretching routines.
  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training).
